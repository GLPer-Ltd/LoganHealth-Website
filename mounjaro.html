Mounjaro for Weight Loss

A breakthrough dual-hormone weight loss treatment that targets both GLP-1 and GIP receptors for enhanced results

What is Mounjaro?

Mounjaro is a prescription-only injectable medication delivered via a self-administered pen designed for weekly use. The active ingredient is tirzepatide, classified as a GLP-1/GIP receptor agonist.

This innovative treatment represents the latest advancement in weight loss medication, offering a dual-hormone approach that distinguishes it from traditional single-hormone treatments.

How Mounjaro Works

Mounjaro works by targeting two key digestive hormones—GLP-1 and GIP—to curb your appetite and regulate your blood sugar levels. This dual-hormone approach is what makes Mounjaro particularly effective.

Appetite Control: Reduces hunger and helps you feel fuller for longer

Blood Sugar Regulation: Improves insulin sensitivity and glucose control

Dual Hormone Action: Activates both GLP-1 and GIP receptors for enhanced effect

Clinical Effectiveness

Proven Results

Clinical studies show Mounjaro can help reduce body weight by up to 22.5% when combined with diet and exercise, compared to just 3% with lifestyle changes alone.

What to Expect

  • First Month: Most people lose approximately 4% of body weight during the initial month on the starting dose of 2.5mg
  • Progressive Results: Weight loss accelerates as doses increase over time
  • Long-term Success: Maximum effectiveness achieved with consistent use and lifestyle modifications

Who Can Use Mounjaro?

Standard eligibility criteria include:

  • Individuals with a BMI of 30 or higher
  • OR BMI of 27 or higher with weight-related health conditions

Note: Private providers may prescribe at lower BMIs if clinical judgment supports the benefit-to-risk ratio for individual patients.

Dosing Information

Starting Dose: 2.5mg weekly injection

Pen Capacity: Each pen contains four weekly doses

Supply Cycle: New pens sent every four weeks

Maintenance: Personalized dose determined by your clinical team

Missed a Dose?

If 4 days or fewer have passed: Take the missed dose immediately

If more than 4 days have passed: Skip that dose and resume your regular schedule

Side Effects & Management

Common Side Effects

Most people experience mild to moderate gastrointestinal effects, including:

  • Nausea
  • Loose stools or constipation
  • Digestive discomfort

Timeline: Side effects typically appear within days of your first injection or dose increases, with improvement within 2-4 weeks as your body adjusts.

Tips for Managing Side Effects

Start with low doses and increase gradually

Maintain a simple diet during dose adjustments

Stay well hydrated throughout the day

Eat smaller portions more frequently

Avoid trigger foods (fatty, spicy, acidic items)

Limit alcohol and caffeine consumption

Keep a consistent injection schedule

Mounjaro vs Wegovy

Both medications are highly effective for weight loss, but there are some key differences:

Feature Mounjaro Wegovy
Active Ingredient Tirzepatide Semaglutide
Mechanism Dual-hormone (GLP-1 + GIP) Single-hormone (GLP-1)
Maximum Weight Loss Up to 22.5% at 15mg dose Up to 20.7% at maximum dose
Cardiac Benefits Demonstrated in studies Licensed for cardiovascular protection

Bottom line: Mounjaro typically delivers slightly greater weight loss due to its dual-hormone mechanism, while both medications have demonstrated cardiovascular benefits.

Important Considerations

Fertility & Pregnancy

Mounjaro can improve fertility in women with PCOS through weight loss and improved insulin sensitivity. However:

  • The medication is unsafe during pregnancy
  • Must stop at least one month before attempting conception
  • Mounjaro reduces oral contraceptive effectiveness
  • Use non-oral contraception methods (IUD, implant) or barrier protection (condoms)
  • Additional contraception needed for 4 weeks after starting treatment and each dose increase

Weight Maintenance

Weight regain is common if you stop taking Mounjaro without maintaining the lifestyle changes built during treatment. Your appetite will gradually return to normal over several weeks as the medication clears your system.

Our support includes: Guidance on maintaining your weight loss through sustainable lifestyle habits even after treatment ends.

How Quickly Does It Work?

Mounjaro starts working within a few hours of your first injection, but it takes time to notice the effects:

  • Some people experience reduced appetite immediately
  • Others may require several weeks to notice changes
  • Effects become increasingly noticeable as doses escalate

Safety Profile

Mounjaro has demonstrated a strong safety profile through extensive clinical trials and regulatory review. It has been approved by the UK's MHRA (Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency) for both weight management and type 2 diabetes treatment.
