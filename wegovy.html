Wegovy for Weight Loss

A proven GLP-1 weight loss treatment that helps you feel fuller for longer and achieve sustainable results

What is Wegovy?

Wegovy is a prescription injectable medication designed for weekly self-administration. The active ingredient is semaglutide, delivered through pre-filled pens that make the injection process simple and convenient.

As one of the most well-studied GLP-1 medications for weight loss, Wegovy has helped thousands of people achieve significant, sustainable weight reduction when combined with a healthy diet and regular exercise.

How Wegovy Works

Wegovy's active ingredient, semaglutide, mimics the body's natural GLP-1 hormone to support weight loss in multiple ways:

Appetite Control: Reduces hunger sensations and helps you feel fuller for longer

Craving Reduction: Decreases cravings for unhealthy, high-calorie foods

Slower Digestion: Helps food stay in your stomach longer, extending fullness

Blood Sugar Regulation: Helps regulate blood sugar levels for better metabolic health

Set Point Reduction: Lowers your body's weight "set point" for sustained weight loss

Clinical Effectiveness

Proven Results

Standard dose (2.4mg): Supports approximately 17.5% body weight reduction

Higher dose (7.2mg): Clinical studies show up to 20.7% weight loss

What to Expect

  • First Month: Typical results show around 2% of starting weight loss
  • Progressive Results: Significant appetite suppression increases as dosage increases
  • Maximum Effect: Achieved at months 3-6 when at higher doses
  • Best Combined: Results optimize when paired with diet and exercise

Dosing Schedule

Wegovy follows a gradual titration schedule to minimize side effects and allow your body to adjust:

Standard Titration Schedule

Month 1: 0.25mg weekly

Month 2: 0.5mg weekly

Month 3: 1mg weekly

Month 4: 1.7mg weekly

Month 5+: 2.4mg weekly (maintenance)

Note: You can maintain at a lower dose if you prefer, rather than advancing to the maximum dose.

Injection Sites

Wegovy can be injected in three areas (rotate sites to minimize discomfort):

  • Abdomen: At least 5cm from belly button
  • Thighs: Front or outer areas
  • Upper arms: Back or outer areas

Missed a Dose?

Within 5 days: Take immediately and resume regular schedule

More than 5 days: Skip that dose and continue with scheduled injections

2+ weeks missed: Contact your clinical team; may need to restart at lower dose

Who Can Use Wegovy?

Standard Eligibility

  • BMI of 30 or higher
  • OR BMI of 27 or higher with weight-related conditions (high blood pressure, type 2 diabetes, high cholesterol)

Who Should NOT Use Wegovy

Wegovy is not suitable if you have:

Pregnancy, breastfeeding, or actively trying to conceive

Active cancer requiring ongoing treatment

Liver, kidney, or heart failure

Pancreatitis or pancreas inflammation

Type 1 diabetes or diabetic retinopathy

History of eating disorders

Gallbladder problems or gallstones

Inflammatory bowel disease or gastroparesis

Personal/family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or MEN 2

Side Effects & Management

Common Side Effects

Most people experience digestive-related side effects, particularly:

  • Nausea
  • Diarrhea
  • Constipation

Timeline: Side effects typically appear within the first few days after injection or dose increases, then resolve within 2-4 weeks as your body adjusts.

Tips for Managing Side Effects

Keep meals light and simple during dose adjustments

Eat smaller portions more frequently

Stay well hydrated throughout the day

Avoid fatty, greasy, or spicy foods

Limit alcohol consumption

Avoid large meals that overload your digestive system

Important Considerations

Fertility & Pregnancy

Wegovy may improve fertility in some women, especially those with PCOS, by supporting weight loss and improved insulin sensitivity. However:

  • Critical requirement: Must discontinue at least 2 months before attempting pregnancy
  • Reliable contraception is mandatory during treatment
  • Wegovy is not safe to use during pregnancy or while breastfeeding

Weight Maintenance After Stopping

When discontinuing Wegovy, your appetite will gradually return to normal within weeks as the medication clears your system. Weight regain is possible without maintaining the lifestyle changes built during treatment.

Good news: Many people successfully maintain their weight loss through the healthy dietary and exercise habits they developed during treatment. You can also restart treatment if needed.

How Quickly Does It Work?

Wegovy starts working in your body within hours of your first injection, but it takes a few weeks before you'll notice real changes:

  • Weeks 1-2: Some experience reduced hunger; others notice minimal difference
  • Months 3-6: Most significant weight loss occurs at higher doses
  • Key to success: Weekly injections, good nutrition, and regular activity

Wegovy vs Mounjaro

Both medications are highly effective for weight loss, each with unique benefits:

Feature Wegovy Mounjaro
Active Ingredient Semaglutide Tirzepatide
Mechanism Single-hormone (GLP-1) Dual-hormone (GLP-1 + GIP)
Maximum Weight Loss Up to 20.7% at higher dose Up to 22.5% at 15mg dose
Cardiovascular Protection Licensed for CV benefits Demonstrated in studies

Bottom line: While Mounjaro shows slightly higher weight loss due to its dual-hormone mechanism, Wegovy holds exclusive licensing for cardiovascular protection and has a longer track record of clinical use.

Safety Profile

Wegovy has undergone extensive clinical trials and has been approved by both the FDA (US) and MHRA (UK) for weight management. It has been used by thousands of patients worldwide with a well-established safety profile.
